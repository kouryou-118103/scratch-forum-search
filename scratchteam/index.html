Dear Scratch Team,

My Commitment to Safety and Compliance

Data Management and Community Safety

To protect the community and avoid indexing deleted content, my search engine only includes closed major topics or posts older than three months.

This approach ensures that I do not conflict with Scratch Team's moderation policies or inadvertently display content that should be removed.

Low Server Impact

This system does not fetch data from Scratch servers in real time. By relying on pre-indexed data and avoiding frequent requests, I minimize the load on Scratch's infrastructure and ensure my project is a respectful addition to the community.

I deeply respect the Scratch community and aim to provide a tool that supports its core values.
